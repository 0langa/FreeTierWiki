## Why it is worth using on free tier

Cloudflare Workers excels for globally distributed APIs and request shaping because the free tier is large enough for most hobby and early-stage production systems.

## Operational notes

- Keep bundles small for faster deploy and startup.
- Use KV and Durable Objects selectively; they can change performance profile.
- Track per-route usage to avoid unexpected daily cap exhaustion.
